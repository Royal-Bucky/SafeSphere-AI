# 🛡️ SafeSphere AI

> **Predict. Prevent. Protect.**

SafeSphere AI is an AI-powered predictive women safety platform that proactively identifies potential danger before an emergency occurs. Instead of relying solely on manual SOS activation, SafeSphere continuously analyzes contextual signals such as location, movement, route deviations, and voice cues to detect risky situations and automatically initiate emergency protocols when required.

---

## 🌟 Overview

Traditional safety applications depend on users manually pressing an emergency button. However, in many real-life situations, victims may not have the opportunity to unlock their phone or activate an SOS alert.

SafeSphere AI addresses this limitation through intelligent, context-aware monitoring that enables proactive emergency detection.

The platform combines mobile sensing, Artificial Intelligence, and real-time communication to provide a safer travel experience for women during daily commutes.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

- 📍 Real-time GPS & route monitoring
- 🤖 AI-powered contextual risk prediction
- 🎤 Voice emotion & distress detection
- 🚶 Motion pattern anomaly detection
- 🗺️ Route deviation analysis
- ⚡ Dynamic AI Risk Scoring
- ✅ Human confirmation ("Are you Safe?")
- 🚨 Automatic emergency response
- 📡 Live location sharing
- 🔒 Privacy-first data handling
- 📝 Secure evidence timeline generation
